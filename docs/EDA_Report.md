# International Debt Analysis

## Objective
Build a complete Python, SQL, and dashboard analytics project using World Bank International Debt Statistics.

## Dataset
Source: https://databank.worldbank.org/source/international-debt-statistics
Processed years: 2010 to 2022

## Key Metrics
- Main year: 2022
- Processed rows: 238,079
- Countries: 120
- Indicators: 243
- Total external debt: $8,682.57B
- Highest-debt country: China
- Lowest-debt country: Tonga

## Insights
- In 2022, total external debt across the analysed countries was $8,682.57B.
- China had the highest total external debt at $2,530.50B.
- The top 10 countries accounted for 68.75% of total external debt, showing a high concentration among a small group of borrowers.
- East Asia & Pacific was the largest regional contributor with $3,496.64B, or 40.27% of the analysed total.
- From 2010 to 2022, total external debt changed by 126.22%, indicating the longer-term direction of global debt exposure.

## Deliverables
- Cleaned CSV dataset and normalized tables.
- MySQL schema and 30 analytical SQL queries.
- Streamlit dashboard.
- Final DOCX and PDF report.
